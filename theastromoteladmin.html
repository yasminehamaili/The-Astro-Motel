Welcome To Astro Motel!

Nestled in the heart of Bejaia, our motel combines the charm of Algeria's stunning coastline with the comfort and warmth of home. Whether you're here for business, leisure, or a quick getaway, we promise an unforgettable stay.

Discover our Motel

What make us special?

Scenic Views

Enjoy stunning views of Bejaia's coastline and lush green mountains, right from your room or our outdoor terrace.

Proximity to Attractions

Located near top landmarks like the Yemma Gouraya National Park, Cap Carbon Lighthouse, and Bejaia's pristine beaches.

Comfortable Accommodations

Rooms designed for relaxation, featuring modern amenities such as air conditioning, high-speed Wi-Fi, and premium bedding.

Delicious Local Cuisine

Our on-site restaurant serves authentic Algerian dishes and fresh seafood, giving you a true taste of Bejaia.

Warm and Friendly Service

A team dedicated to making your stay unforgettable, offering personalized recommendations and 24/7 assistance.

Affordable Luxury

Enjoy exceptional quality and comfort at competitive rates, making your stay both memorable and budget-friendly.

Our motel offers unparalleled views of Bejaia’s stunning coastline and lush mountains, creating a serene backdrop for your stay. Whether enjoying your morning coffee on a private balcony or watching the sunset from our terrace, you’ll be immersed in the natural beauty that makes Bejaia a hidden gem of Algeria.
